**Title :- Outpatient Department (OPD) Administartion Management System** 



**Description :-** 

**"An Outpatient Department (OPD) Administration Management System is a software solution designed to manage and streamline the daily operations of a hospital’s outpatient services. It helps in handling patient registration, appointment scheduling, doctor allocation, and maintaining digital medical records. The system improves efficiency by reducing manual work, minimizing errors, and ensuring quick access to patient information. Overall, it enhances patient care, saves time, and makes hospital management more organized and effective."**
